## 1. Contribution record

Each dataset, file, or collection submitted under this Agreement is a **Contribution** and must
have an attached Amosclaud Dataset Rights Schedule identifying its source, owner, collection
method, applicable notices, personal-data status, and content hash. Amosclaud may reject any
Contribution.

## 2. Ownership

Contributor retains ownership of the Contribution. No ownership transfers to Amosclaud except
for rights expressly granted below. Amosclaud owns its models, software, configurations,
evaluations, improvements, and other technology, excluding the original Contribution.

## 3. License grant

Contributor grants Amosclaud and its affiliates a worldwide, non-exclusive, royalty-free,
fully paid, transferable, and sublicensable license, for the duration of the applicable rights,
to:

1. host, copy, reproduce, clean, label, filter, translate, annotate, tokenize, transform, combine,
   and create derivative datasets from the Contribution;
2. use the Contribution to research, develop, train, fine-tune, test, evaluate, secure, monitor,
   and improve artificial-intelligence and machine-learning systems;
3. use service providers and infrastructure vendors to perform those activities under
   confidentiality and data-protection obligations;
4. deploy, distribute, license, sell, and otherwise commercialize models, model weights,
   adapters, embeddings, evaluations, and outputs developed using the Contribution; and
5. preserve records needed for security, provenance, legal compliance, and reproducibility.

Amosclaud will not sell or redistribute the original Contribution as a standalone dataset unless
the Dataset Rights Schedule explicitly authorizes that use.

## 4. Contributor promises

Contributor represents and warrants that:

- Contributor owns the Contribution or has written authority to grant every right in this
  Agreement;
- the Contribution and its collection do not violate copyright, contract, confidentiality,
  privacy, publicity, database, employment, or other third-party rights;
- all required permissions, notices, and consents have been obtained and documented;
- the Contribution contains no credentials, private keys, passwords, malware, regulated health
  information, payment-card data, biometric identifiers, precise location data, or data from
  children unless Amosclaud expressly approves it in writing under a separate agreement;
- license notices, attribution information, opt-outs, and usage restrictions have not been
  removed or concealed; and
- all information in the Dataset Rights Schedule is accurate and complete.

## 5. Personal data and removal requests

Contributor must identify any personal data before submission. Amosclaud may delete, quarantine,
or decline affected material. Valid privacy rights and legally required deletion requests remain
applicable. Because trained parameters do not ordinarily preserve a retrievable copy of a source
record, removal from future training corpora may not technically remove all influence from models
already trained, except where law or a separate written commitment requires additional action.

## 6. Moral rights and attribution

To the extent permitted by law, Contributor waives and agrees not to assert moral rights that
would prevent the licensed uses. Required attribution and notices must be listed in the Dataset
Rights Schedule; Amosclaud will preserve them in its provenance records where reasonably
practicable.

## 7. Confidentiality

Contributor must not submit confidential material unless a separate written confidentiality
agreement authorizes it. Amosclaud does not accept confidentiality obligations merely because a
Contribution is marked confidential.

## 8. Compensation

Unless a signed Dataset Rights Schedule states otherwise, Contributions are voluntary and no fee,
royalty, model access, credit, employment, or other compensation is owed.

## 9. Withdrawal and termination

Contributor may request that Amosclaud stop using a Contribution in future training runs by
contacting **[DATA RIGHTS EMAIL]** and providing the contribution ID and proof of authority.
Amosclaud will process valid requests according to law and the applicable schedule. Termination
does not revoke rights already exercised in models, evaluations, backups, or distributed products,
except as required by law or expressly agreed in writing.

## 10. Disclaimer and liability

The Contribution is provided **AS IS**. Any warranties, indemnities, liability limits, and claim
procedures applicable to organizational contributors must be stated in an executed Dataset Rights
Schedule. Consumer rights that cannot legally be waived remain unaffected.

## 11. General terms

This Agreement and each executed schedule are the complete agreement regarding the Contribution.
Changes must be in writing. If a provision is unenforceable, the remainder survives. Neither party
may use the other party's trademarks without permission. Governing law and venue are **[GOVERNING
LAW AND EXCLUSIVE VENUE]**. Electronic acceptance and signatures are permitted.
