Subject to payment and compliance, Amosclaud grants Customer a non-exclusive, non-transferable
license during the Term to:

1. store and reproduce the Dataset within Customer's controlled systems;
2. clean, label, filter, annotate, tokenize, transform, and combine the Dataset for the Permitted

3. train, fine-tune, test, evaluate, and secure Customer's artificial-intelligence systems; and
4. deploy and commercialize resulting models, weights, adapters, and outputs, if the Order Form
